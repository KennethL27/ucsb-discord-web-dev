Welcome to UCSB Physics Discord

For each physics course taught at UCSB we have a dedicated message and voice channels. Here you can talk to classmates, TAs, LAs, and even some Professors!.

We also strive to bring important announcments to the physics community. Announcments can range from course updates, club events, department news, general UCSB news, etc.

We also foster one of the primary resources for students to ask questions about the physics department, courses, or major. These questions can be answered almost immediately for any one of our active peers.

Since the start of this Discord Server we have been growing and evolving, we hope you can join us in creating a beautiful physics community here at UCSB Physics Discord.
